Request an emergency repair

An emergency repair is when there's immediate danger to you or the structure of the building. In an emergency we'll make the situation safe; we may need to return another day to complete the full repair.

All emergency repairs can be reported 24 hours a day, 7 days a week by calling 0800 952 4444 or 020 7525 2600. You may experience longer waiting times due to a high volume of calls.
